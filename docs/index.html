ABOUT

Welcome to a 3D puzzle platformer where logic is your key to progression. Navigate the environment by pressing buttons to manipulate wires and activate logic nodes (AND, OR, NOT). Solve the circuit puzzles to alter the platforms' trajectories, change their states, and forge your path forward!
